Ensuring child protection welfare among marginalized communities in Rautahat

Ensuring child protection welfare among marginalized communities in Rautahat is a paramount endeavor that our school is committed to undertaking.
Recognizing the vulnerabilities faced by children from disadvantaged backgrounds, our institution has taken proactive steps to create a safe and nurturing environment for all students. Through targeted outreach programs and community engagement initiatives, we aim to identify and address the unique challenges that children from marginalized communities encounter. Our school has established partnerships with local organizations and government agencies to implement comprehensive child protection policies, providing workshops and training to teachers, parents, and community members. By fostering a culture of inclusivity and empathy, we strive to empower every child to flourish academically, emotionally, and socially, irrespective of their socio-economic background. Together, with unwavering dedication, we endeavor to build a brighter future for the children of Rautahat, where their well-being and growth are safeguarded and nurtured.
