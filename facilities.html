Testimonials

Our Facilities

Library

Our good library houses an extensive collection of books, periodicals, reference materials, and digital resources, catering to a wide range of interests and academic pursuits. Whether students are seeking fictional adventures, historical insights, scientific knowledge, or artistic inspiration, our library offers a diverse selection that sparks curiosity and fosters a love for reading and learning. Our dedicated library staff are always ready to assist students in locating materials, conducting research, and honing their information literacy skills.

Large Sports Ground

We are proud to offer our students a large sports ground that serves as a hub for physical activity, teamwork, and healthy competition. Our expansive sports ground provides ample space for a wide range of sports and recreational activities, catering to the diverse interests and talents of our students. Whether it's soccer, basketball, cricket, athletics, or any other sport, our well-maintained facilities encourage students to participate, stay active, and develop essential life skills such as discipline, resilience, and sportsmanship.

Canteen

We prioritize the quality and hygiene of the food served, with a dedicated team of skilled cooks and staff who are committed to delivering meals that are both tasty and wholesome. From hot and freshly prepared meals to a variety of snacks, beverages, and dietary options, our canteen strives to create a welcoming space where individuals can socialize, relax, and refuel during break times. Our commitment to maintaining a good canteen reflects our dedication to the overall well-being and satisfaction of our school community.

Science Laboratory

We have a science lab in our well-equipped science lab, we foster an environment that promotes curiosity, discovery, and hands-on learning. The lab is designed to facilitate various scientific experiments, research projects, and practical demonstrations. Our aim is to encourage students, researchers, and scientists to explore the wonders of the natural world through experimentation and analysis.

Hostel

Welcome to our comfortable and welcoming hostel, designed to provide a home away from home for travelers and students alike. Situated in a convenient location, our hostel offers a safe and inviting environment for individuals from all walks of life. Whether you're a backpacker exploring the world or a student pursuing your academic dreams, our hostel is the perfect place to stay.
